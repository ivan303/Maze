Here you can add your maze.

With the selector below you can choose dimension of your maze.
By clicking appropriate field you'll change wall (1) to corridor (0)
We assume that there are only two fields on borders of maze with 0 - entrance and exit.
Entrance and exit can't be in corners and can't be connected directly.
It must be exactly one way from entrance to exit.
There may not be chambers - four fields with zeros making square.
There may not be cycles (loops) in maze.
